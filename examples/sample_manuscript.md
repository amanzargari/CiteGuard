## Related Work

Transformer architectures have fundamentally changed natural language processing [1].
The attention mechanism enables models to weigh token relationships dynamically.

Large language models trained on internet-scale corpora demonstrate emergent
capabilities not present at smaller scales (Brown et al., 2020). Fine-tuning on
task-specific data further improves performance.

Recent work has shown that retrieval-augmented generation improves factual accuracy
significantly[2]. Combining dense retrieval with generative models reduces hallucination.

The BERT model achieved state-of-the-art results on eleven NLP tasks [devlin2019].
Bidirectional pre-training allows BERT to capture rich contextual representations.
